About me

Welcome!

Angelica Zhiyu Luo is a bilingual journalist pursuing her master’s degree at Northwestern University. Prior to that, she attended Vassar College and graduated Summer 2024. She writes about fashion, entertainment and culture, sometimes with an anthropological or critical twist. Her works were previously featured digitally in L’OFFICIEL China and Harper’s Bazaar China.
