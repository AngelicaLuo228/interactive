As Los Angeles residents fled the raging wildfire, many were forced to leave pets behind. Rescue-foster organization Friends of Normie is there to help.

Founded in 2020 and based in So-Cal, Friends of Normie specializes in (re)homing cats and kitties. Under current circumstances, it pivoted to find sanctuaries for all animals. So far, they have saved over 100 cats!
